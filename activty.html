Redragon IRE Pro Headphones

Features

3 Mode Connection

The mode of connection in gaming headsets is a crucial aspect that can significantly impact your overall gaming experience. The Redragon 3-mode wireless gaming headset, marked as H848, stands out in this regard, as it offers a 2.4Ghz receiver along with low latency Bluetooth connectivity. This combination enables gamers to enjoy more freedom while playing on different devices, including PCs, smartphones, and PlayStations.

Precise Clear Audio

Experience the authentic and lossless audio quality with the cutting-edge 7.1 Surround-Sound technology, which is capable of creating an immersive sound field, be it while gaming, attending a live concert, or participating in a virtual chat room. The 40mm drivers embedded in the sound system provide a wider frequency range, richer sound fields, higher definition, and an exceptional level of sound fidelity that is unmatched in terms of quality and clarity.

DETACHABLE MICROPHONE

The detachable microphone can significantly enhance the quality of communication among teammates. By incorporating noise-cancellation technology, it ensures a clear transmission of sound, free from any distortions or boomed mic cases. This feature can prove to be a valuable asset for those who prioritize effective communication in their professional or personal endeavors.
